How To Use It

Learn all you need to know about Crime Reporter

  1. Panic Mode

    Panic mode is the state where Crime Reporter is active and ready for you to instruct it as to what to do.
    To enter into panic mode turn your phones screen on three (3) times by pressing the power button in rapid succession. If Crime Reporter enters into panic mode it will notify you by vibrating your phone.
    If you accidentally turn activate Crime Reporter, just wait for half a secound and turn on your phone screen again to leave panic mode.

  2. Sending a Panic Message

    To send a panic message just shake your phone while it is in panic mode.
    It will vibrate when panic message it sent.
    Note: Your GPS needs to be on. Since your location is sent with the message.

  3. Making a Secrete Recording

    While in panic mode press any of the volume buttons.
    To stop the recording press any of the volume buttons again.
    Note: Recording can only be stopped after 1 second of recording.

  4. Doing Both

    To send panic message and also start audio recording,
    Send the message first and then start the recording.
    Note: The other way round will not work.
